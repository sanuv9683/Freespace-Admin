We collect certain types of data necessary for the functionality of the FreeSpace Toolkit app, including:

Information collected is used solely for enhancing and optimizing the user experience, including:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Access to your information is restricted to authorized personnel only, and all data transmissions are encrypted to prevent unauthorized access.

All data processed within the FreeSpace Toolkit is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the app's intended functionalities. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting our support team.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal data or Excel sheet content with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.
